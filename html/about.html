About

My previous experience mainly lies within the realm of higher education/student affairs and nonprofit work. While I loved being student-facing in my work, I was always curious about how I could apply myself in web development.

After some time in the education sphere and obtaining my Masters degree, I realized that I was in dire need of an actual break due to burnout. After this break, I saw that there were bootcamp programs that would allow me to pivot into this new industry.

I am enrolled in General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Remote program, which will give me the technical skills to become a software engineer in the near future!
